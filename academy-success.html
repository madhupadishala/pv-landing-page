Your enrollment is the first step. The project discovery step helps ClinixAI understand your background, goals and healthcare problem area.

1. Payment Confirmation

2. Project Discovery

You will complete a guided project discovery process where ClinixAI helps identify a healthcare problem, refine your idea and recommend the most suitable AI project path.

3. Welcome Message

You will receive a welcome message from ClinixAI Academy through email and/or WhatsApp with the next steps.
