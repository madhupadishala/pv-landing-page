Prepare Before Your First Session

You do not need to prepare technically. Just think clearly about the healthcare problem you care about.

Think About These Questions

  • What healthcare problem do you want to solve?
  • Who experiences this problem?
  • What happens today without AI?
  • What should your AI tool help with?
  • How much time can you commit each week?
  • Do you prefer no-code only or optional Python learning?
  • How do you want to showcase the project in your career?

Your answers will help us guide your project direction more effectively.
